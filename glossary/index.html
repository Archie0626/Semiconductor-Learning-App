Glossary

  • Diode

    A component that allows current to flow in one direction only.

  • MOSFET

    A field-effect transistor used for switching and amplifying signals.

  • Doping

    Adding impurities to a semiconductor to change its electrical behavior.

  • PN Junction

    The boundary between p-type and n-type semiconductors.

  • Semiconductor

    A material with conductivity between conductors and insulators.
